PUP- PhySoc opens PISOC: Donation Drive Para Sa Mga Future Physicist Ng Bayan

By Armielyn C. Obinguar
Tue, October 13, 2020

With the aim to provide mobile data allowance to Physics students ,the PUP Physics Society (PhySoc), recently launched the PISOC Project, a donation drive last September 18 through G-cash and bank donations.

According to the organization’s Finance chairperson Kaye Nepomuceno, the fund for the said project came from the extra fund of previous batch of executives, alumni, and other donors who wished to help some members of the organization who needs financial support for education amidst this pandemic.

“Initially we know that not all of us have the privilege to access internet so we thought why not help the others since it will be hard for them if they will be in modular mode. And that’s how the PISOC started” – Nepomuceno said in an interview.

Altogether, the PISOC project have nine beneficiaries, two of whom are chosen by the DM Academy, who will receive mobile data allowance until the end of the first semester of this school year.

“I’m very grateful that PISOC Project is aiming to help young people to pursue their dreams. With the help of PISOC, we can now cope up with the new system of education since I come from an economically challenged family. PISOC Project is a well-founded plan for action to give support to others” – said sophomore Freedom Delacruz.

As of this October 9, 2020, the PISOC Project have successfully raised an overall funds of PHP 5,801.97 worth of donations in partnership with the alumni and students.

“In this first week of classes, many of the beneficiaries have attended their orientation with the use of the mobile data that is given to them and I am hoping that we can pull this project off and reach more students in our society.” – Kaye Nepomuceno added to her interview.
